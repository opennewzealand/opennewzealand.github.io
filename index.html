What We Do

We do things on the web that improve interactions between government and citizens.

Web technology enables better interactions between government and citizens. Government can inform, consult, and serve citizens better through the web. Citizens can use, check, and improve the services of their government.

Government departments can work better together, reducing waste. Citizens can efficiently work together, reducing the need for government services.

What We Want

We want to see a world where things citizens want are communicated clearly to government.

Where there are greater economic, social and cultural opportunities for citizens.

And where government operates more efficiently and transparently.
